Xiao Wang (王晓)

 

I am currently an Associate Professor at Pengcheng Laboratory. Before joining Pengcheng Laboratory, I was an associate professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. I obtained my Ph.D. (Supervised by Prof. Ya-xiang Yuan) from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2012 and received my bachelor's degree from Shandong University in 2007.
Email: wangx07@pcl.ac.cn

Research Interests
