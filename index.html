Xiao Wang (王晓)

 

I am currently an Associate Professor at Pengcheng Laboratory (PCL). I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2012 and my B.S. degree from the School of Mathematics at Shandong University in 2007. Before joining PCL in November 2021, I had been working in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences since my Ph.D. graduation.
