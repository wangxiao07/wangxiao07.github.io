Xiao Wang (王晓)

 

I am currently a Professor of Mathematics at Sun Yat-sen University. I graduated from Shandong University (BSc) and AMSS of Chinese Academy of Sciences (PhD). Before joining SYSU, I used to work in the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and Pengcheng National Laboratory. My research interest lies in theories, algorithms and applications relavent to mathematical optimization, in particular stochastic optimization, large scale nonconvex optimization and constrained optimization.
